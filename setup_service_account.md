# Service Account Setup - 2 minutes

1. Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/iam-admin/serviceaccounts
2. Select your project (Medikro Sheets MCP)
3. Click **+ CREATE SERVICE ACCOUNT**
4. Name: `medikro-sheets`
5. Click **CREATE AND CONTINUE** → **DONE**
6. Click on the new service account row
7. Go to **KEYS** tab
8. **ADD KEY** → **Create new key** → **JSON** → **CREATE**
9. Move downloaded file to: /Users/mila/video-translator/service-account.json

Then share your spreadsheet with the service account email!
